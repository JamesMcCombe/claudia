## What is Claudia?

CLAUDIA is an **AI receptionist SaaS** for Latin American businesses. Spanish-first, bilingual-ready, with a focus on dental clinics, salons, and small service businesses.

**The Problem:**  
LATAM businesses miss calls, lose customers, and can't afford full-time receptionists.

**The Solution:**  
AI receptionist that answers calls 24/7, schedules appointments, and speaks Spanish fluently (with English toggle).
