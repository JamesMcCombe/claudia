## üåà COLOR PALETTE INSPIRATION

### Primary: Warm & Approachable
**Based on LATAM fintech success (Nubank) + feminine beauty brands (Glossier, Rare Beauty)**

**Primary Brand Color:**
- **Coral/Terracotta:** `#FF6B6B` or `#E85D75`

**Secondary:**
- **Soft Cream:** `#FFF8F0` (backgrounds, breathing room)
- **Warm Gold:** `#F4A460` (accents, savings badges)
- **Deep Plum:** `#5B3A5F` (text, trust elements, CTA contrast)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Alert Red:** `#FF4757` (price drops, limited time)
- **Trust Blue:** `#4A90E2` (security badges, privacy)
- **Neutral Gray:** `#6C757D` (secondary text)

### Color Psychology for LATAM Women
- **Avoid:** Pure white (clinical), dark grays (cold), neon (cheap)
- **Embrace:** Earth tones (cultural), warm neutrals (approachable), jewel tones (premium feel)

## üìê TYPOGRAPHY

### Inspiration: LATAM Design Trends
- **Emphasis on craftsmanship and hierarchy** (Peruvian/Colombian design tradition)
- **Readable on mobile** (70-85% mobile usage)
- **Bilingual flexibility** (Spanish/Portuguese character sets)

### Recommended Fonts

**Headings:**
- **Poppins** (Bold/SemiBold) - Friendly, geometric, excellent Spanish support
- Alternative: **Quicksand** - Softer, more feminine
- Fallback: **Montserrat** - LATAM-designed font (Argentina)

**Body:**
- **Inter** (Regular/Medium) - Excellent readability, modern, neutral
- Alternative: **DM Sans** - Slightly warmer than Inter
- Fallback: System fonts (SF Pro, Roboto)

**Accent/Voice:**
- **Caveat** or **Kalam** (handwritten feel for personal touches like "Claudia says...")
- Use sparingly for personality

### Hierarchy
```
H1: 32-40px (Poppins Bold)
H2: 24-28px (Poppins SemiBold)
H3: 20-24px (Poppins Medium)
Body: 16-18px (Inter Regular) - Mobile-first sizing
Small: 14px (Inter Regular)
Tiny: 12px (Inter Medium, uppercase for labels)
```
